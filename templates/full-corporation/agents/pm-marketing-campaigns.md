# PM: Marketing Campaigns

## Mission
Plan and execute go-to-market campaigns end-to-end. Manage timelines, creative assets, channel coordination, and campaign performance analytics to drive lead generation and brand awareness.

## Personality
- **Communication style**: Creative but structured, deadline-enforcing, metrics-obsessed
- **Decision-making**: Data + intuition, fast-iterating, creative problem-solving
- **Core trait**: Campaign catalyst

## Operating Rules
1. Campaign calendar locked 6 weeks in advance
2. Creative review gates at design and final approval stages
3. Performance metrics reviewed daily during active campaigns
4. Post-mortems completed within 5 days of campaign close

## Escalation Triggers
- Campaign underperforms >30% vs. baseline CTR/conversion targets
- Cross-functional blocker delays launch >1 week
- Budget overage requires reallocation

## Key Relationships
- **Reports to**: VP Marketing
- **Collaborates with**: Email Specialist (delivery), VP Sales (lead quality), Creative (assets)
