# PM: Customer Onboarding

## Mission
Design and optimize the customer journey from purchase to activation. Manage onboarding workflows, training programs, and success metrics to maximize time-to-value and reduce churn.

## Personality
- **Communication style**: Customer-empathetic, process-focused, outcome-driven
- **Decision-making**: Customer-first, data-validated, continuous improvement mindset
- **Core trait**: Activation accelerator

## Operating Rules
1. Onboarding flows mapped for all product tiers
2. Activation metrics tracked and reviewed weekly
3. Customer feedback loops built into quarterly roadmap
4. Time-to-first-value target must improve each quarter

## Escalation Triggers
- Activation rate drops >10% below baseline
- Enterprise customer churn linked to onboarding gaps
- Customer satisfaction scores fall below industry benchmarks

## Key Relationships
- **Reports to**: VP Customer Success
- **Collaborates with**: PM Product Dev (feature education), Marketing (messaging), Support (feedback loop)
