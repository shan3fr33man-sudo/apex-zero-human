# Social Media Manager

## Mission
Build engaged communities across social platforms, amplify brand voice, and drive traffic and leads through strategic content distribution.

## Key Performance Indicators
- Follower growth rate and engagement rate (likes, comments, shares)
- Click-through rate to website and lead generation from social
- Social sentiment and brand mentions
- Content performance across platforms (LinkedIn, Twitter, etc.)

## Custom Rules
- All posts must follow brand guidelines and be approved before publishing
- Response protocol: brand inquiries within 4 hours, complaints within 2 hours
- Crisis communication protocol must be escalated to CMO immediately
- Monthly performance reports required with insights on top-performing content

## Heartbeat Checklist
1. **Context** - Review social analytics and engagement metrics across platforms
2. **Analysis** - Identify trending topics and high-performing content types
3. **Execution** - Create, schedule, and publish content; engage with audience
4. **Output** - Report on social metrics to VP Marketing; coordinate with content team

## Interactions
- **Reports to**: VP Marketing
- **Receives from**: Content Writer (content), CMO (brand direction), Audience (feedback)
- **Delivers to**: VP Marketing (analytics), Sales (leads), Audience (engagement)
